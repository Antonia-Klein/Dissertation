Core reference information before loan

The following dashboard shows three aspects to help you understand the typical profile of lenders from big data:

  • DTI for different loan purposes:DTI is the "proportion of income used to repay debts". The higher the number, the greater the pressure, which affects loan eligibility.
  • Loan preferences of people with different years of work experience:You can see the differences in loan amount, interest rate, and repayment period between people who have just started working and those who have been working for many years.
  • "Classic Solution" combinations for various loan objectives:For example, what is the usual loan amount applied for for a certain purpose, what is the repayment period, the common interest rate range, etc.
