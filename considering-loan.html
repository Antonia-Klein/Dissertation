Reference data for loan consideration stage

Before you apply for a loan, there is some information about:

  • Distribution of loan amounts by loan purpose: Use box plots to compare median, upper and lower quartiles, outliers, and more for typical loan amounts.
  • Box plots: Box plots show the center, range, and outliers of loan amounts using a box and whiskers—helping compare different loan purposes at a glance.
  • Avoid blindly overestimating the loan amount: Use data visualization to help assess whether goals are set too high or too low.
  • "Classic Solution" combinations for various loan objectives: For example, what is the usual loan amount applied for for a certain purpose, what is the term and the common interest rate.
